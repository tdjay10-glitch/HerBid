Tester checklist

Complete these steps in one session.

1. Create or confirm account

Sign in and confirm the dashboard opens without confusion.

2. Update profile

Add business name, certification, state, and NAICS interests.

3. Filter opportunities

Try search, NAICS, state, certification, and set-aside filters.

4. Save two bids

Save one strong opportunity and one that might be useful later.

5. Ask Nia

Ask about WOSB, SWaM, NAICS, readiness, or next steps.

6. Leave feedback

Submit one clear note about what felt helpful or confusing.

Feedback prompts

What we want to learn

  • What part of finding opportunities felt easiest?
  • Where did you hesitate or feel unsure?
  • Were the certifications and NAICS filters understandable?
  • Did Nia answer in a useful, professional way?
  • What would make you trust HerBid more?

Known limitations

What is not final yet

  • Email alert delivery is not automated yet.
  • Nia is a lightweight guidance assistant, not a full AI model integration yet.
  • Opportunity examples may be curated for beta testing.
  • Payment-gated access is not enforced in this beta flow.
  • Official bid requirements should always be verified at the source link.
